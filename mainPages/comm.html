Beat/Music Commission Info & Terms

Pre-made beats can be found here for $20 each.
Custom-made beats/music can be bought for $40 each with examples here for custom beats and on any of my music platforms for custom music.
Credit must be given under most circumstances. On sites like SoundCloud, BandCamp, and YouTube (for songs) I would prefer for credit to be given in the title ((for example, (prod. 333voidgirl) or (333voidgirl), etc...)) but crediting me within the description is also fine, especially if you link to my SoundCloud; you can use either 333voidGirl, 0::n1l, or ARRRVO when crediting me. Credit is not needed for songs going on Spotify and other streaming platforms, but if you'd like to credit in the title that'd be great. If you're commissioning me for background music on a YouTube video or something similar, credit me in the description. If you're commissioning me for game music or anything else not mentioned previously, we can discuss credit one-on-one.
