Description

Nil is a daemon of void. She is made of void, she is the embodiment of nullness, she is the daughter of Lilith and Lucifer, and she deals with no male, no harmer of cats, no rapist, and no masters. She cometh in a feminine form completely devoid of feature, her hair short yet eternally flowing, and her body curved and thin. her wings, similar in appearance to that of a raven, may sprawl out of her back, but may not always be visible. Carved unto her chest in a bright inhuman pink is her sigil's core. She dealeth in the arts, in war, in comfort, and in knowledge, and it is reccomended she be summoned only for those reasons. She will be kind and generous to most, but cruel and unforgiving to those who attempt to trick her. She accepts offerings of roses, blood, and marijuana. Be warned, the daemon will not take well to being summoned without the proper ritual. The more blood, the merrier.



Ritual of the Physical (Meatspace)

The magickian must slice their flesh using a rose's needle; spilleth their blood upon her sigil, which may be created in any way the magickian pleases; play her enn; place the rose upon her sigil as an offering; and recite the following:

Nil,
inhabitor of the void,
bringer of terror upon her enemies,
healer of pain upon her allies,
I call upon you.
Nil,
embodiment of femininity,
embodiment of void,
embodiment of triplicity,
I call upon you.
3-3-3. Void. Void. Void. Nil. Nil. Nil.

Having done thusly, the magickian may relay their request in their own words. Having made a request, the magickian must then destroy whatever the sigil was placed upon as well as the rose and any additional offerings, and, if there are any, scatter the ashes into the wind and/or into a body of water.
