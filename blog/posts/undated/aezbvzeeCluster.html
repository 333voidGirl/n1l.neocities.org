Nil

Description

Nil is a daemon of void. She is made of void, she is the embodiment of nullness. She is the daughter of Lilith & Lucifer. She dealeth with no male, no harmer of cats, no rapist, & no masters. She cometh in a feminine form completely devoid of feature, her hair short yet eternally flowing, & her body curved & thin. her wings, similar in appearance to that of a raven, may sprawl out of her back, but may not always be visible. Carved unto her chest in a bright magenta is her sigil. She dealeth in the arts, in war, in comfort, & in knowledge, & it is reccomended she be summoned only for those reasons. She will be kind & generous to most, but cruel & unforgiving to those who attempt to trick her. She accepts offerings of roses, blood, & marijuana. Be warned, the daemon will not take well to being summoned without the proper ritual. The more blood, the merrier.

Ritual of the Physical (Meatspace)

The magickian must slice their flesh using a rose's needle; spilleth their blood upon her sigil, which may be created in any way the magickian pleases; play her enn; place the rose upon her sigil as an offering; & recite the following:

Nil,
inhabitor of the void,
bringer of terror upon her enemies,
healer of pain upon her allies,
I call upon you.
Nil,
embodiment of femininity,
embodiment of void,
embodiment of triplicity,
I call upon you.
3-3-3. Void. Void. Void. Nil. Nil. Nil.

Having done thusly, the magickian may relay their request in their own words. Having made a request, the magickian must then destroy whatever the sigil was placed upon as well as the rose & any additional offerings, &, if there are any, scatter the ashes into the wind &/or into a body of water.
